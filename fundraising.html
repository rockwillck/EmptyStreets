Our Role

Empty Streets is an organization that acts as a bridge between human trafficking shelters and potential donors. Our goal is to facilitate more donations and fundraisers for these shelters. We raise funds by going door-to-door to spread awareness and by selling baked goods.

Donations

We collect donations online (through the donate link in the menu), door-to-door, and on campus. We're working towards our goal, and you can see our progress below - live!
